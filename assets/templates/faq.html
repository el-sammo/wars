    Is My Reservation Fee Refundable?

    Your reservation fee is not refundable, but the cost to purchase the actual ticket ($1) is refundable.
    Do I Choose My Seat Location?

    You don't get to choose your seat at this time. We are, however, working toward this goal.
    How Long Can I Wait To Reserve?

    You can wait until after the opening round of the tournament/playoffs, but the reservation fee will increase throughout the season. It's best to lock in the least expensive reservation fee as soon as possible.
    How Do I Pay My Reservation Fee?

    We accept all major credit/debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, etc.
    How Do You Get The Tickets?

    We source the championship tickets through the secondary ticket exchange markets. In the event that we are unable to source your ticket(s) for any reason, we will simply mail you a check/money order for the full face value of the ticket(s).
    Is This Site Secure?

    This site is safe and secure. All your interactions, including credit/debit card transactions, are secured with the latest SSL security protocols. Additionally, your credit/debit card data is not stored by this site, but rather is encrypted and secured by our payment gateway, Authorize.Net - the industry leader in online payments.
