Tickets Tycoon, Inc.
    TicketsTycoon.com was born when its founders were sitting in a local sports bar watching the Super Bowl a few years ago and decided to save money for a trip to the big game the following year. They knew prices were expensive, but after a quick Google search, they discovered single tickets were selling for a whopping $4,000 on the secondary market. Frustrated tickets were so pricey and there was no way at the time to secure a ticket price for less, the Tickets Tycoon business model was created. After a few years under development and testing, Tickets Tycoon became the first championship sports ticket reservation site in which fans could reserve a ticket for their favorite team for pennies on the dollar. If a fan’s team goes to the championship game, tickets are sent to that fan so they can attend the game - LIVE! Fans can reserve 1, 2, or 4 tickets for most major sporting events for as little as $50!
